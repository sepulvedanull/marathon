St. Jude Memphis Marathon

(2017 overview)

In 2017 St. Jude Children's Research Hospital witnessed 2,347 people complete 26.2 miles through the streets of Memphis, TN. Based on public race result data, these graphics seek to explore the trends amongst these devoted runners.

Who were the marathon runners?

In 2017 the St. Jude Memphis Marathlon had 2,347 runners cross the finish line. A majority of the runners traveled from Tennessee (60%), Mississippi (44%), and Arkansas (40%), while some traveled all the way from provinces in Canada! Out of the 2,347 people who finished 1,284 were male and 1,063 were female.
